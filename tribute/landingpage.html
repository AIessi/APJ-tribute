A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Sir Tribute

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, also known as the "Missile Man of India," made significant contributions to the nation's security and defense programs.

  • Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, India
  • Died on July 27, 2015, in Shillong
  • An Indian scientist and politician
  • Played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs
  • Served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007
